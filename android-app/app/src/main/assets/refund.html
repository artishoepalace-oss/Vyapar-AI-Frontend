Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Cancellation

You may request cancellation from the app's Settings or subscription page. Where supported, cancellation can take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Refund requests

Submit a refund request within 7 days of the charge with the registered email, payment ID, charge date and reason. Duplicate charges, verified technical failures or unauthorized transactions will be investigated.

Non-refundable situations

Refunds may be declined where paid features were substantially used, the request is outside the stated period, or the account violated the terms.

Processing

Approved refunds are returned through the original payment method. Processing time may depend on the payment provider and bank.

Contact

Email with your payment details. Never email an OTP, UPI PIN, full card number or banking password.
